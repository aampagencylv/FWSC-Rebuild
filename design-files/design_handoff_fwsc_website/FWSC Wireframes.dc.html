SAFETY FRAMEWORK
The FWSC Standard for Livery Operations
Aligned with F.S. §327.54 and FWC guidance. Adopted by all certified members.
1. Vessel readiness
Pre-rental inspection checklist · maintenance logs · required equipment (PFDs, fire ext., signals)
2. Renter briefing
Mandatory safety briefing · age & license verification · local hazards map
3. Staff training
CPR/first aid · incident response · annual refreshers
4. Insurance & records
Verified coverage minimums · incident reporting to coalition · audit trail
