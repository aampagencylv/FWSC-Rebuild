Safety framework

The FWSC Standard for Livery Operations

Aligned with F.S. §327.54 and FWC guidance. Adopted by every certified member of the Coalition.

Pre-rental inspection checklist, documented maintenance logs, and required safety equipment aboard every vessel — personal flotation devices, fire extinguisher, and signaling devices. A mandatory safety briefing before every departure, age and boating-license verification per F.S. §327.54, and a local hazards map for the operating waterway. CPR and first aid certification for on-water staff, incident response procedures, and annual refresher training documented with the Coalition. Verified coverage at Coalition minimums, incident reporting to the Coalition within 72 hours, and a complete audit trail available for annual review.
